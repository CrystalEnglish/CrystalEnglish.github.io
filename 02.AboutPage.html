About

Skybox Kicks is an upcoming clothing design service that offers a variety of custom services ranging from hats, shirts, shoes and more. Skybox kicks is ran by Quinton "Q" English, who began customizing and reselling shoes in 2019. Q became dedicated to customizing shoes when he realized he could free hand draw during quarantine at the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic. Since then, Q has been determined to learn new techniques to provide a spectacular quality of work.
